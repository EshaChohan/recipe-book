1stRecipe:

Chicken Corn Soup:

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Corn Soup Masala.
  • 5 Eggs:
    1. 2 Raw Eggs.
    2. 3 Boiled Eggs.
  • Salt.
  • Black Pepper.
  • Sauses:
    • Chilli Sause.
    • Soya Sauce.
    • Vinegar.
  • Boiled Corn.
  • Water (H2O).

Method To Cook:

  1. Take 4 glass of water in a pan.
  2. Add chicken corn soup masala in water and stir continouosly till the boil comes.
  3. Add some salt and black pepper and let the soup to be thick.
  4. Put 2 beaten eggs, mix them properly in soup and let the soup to be cook for 5 mins on low flame.
  5. Turn off the flame.
  6. Take boiled eggs and cut it into small pieces.
  7. At last,Add boiled eggs,boiled corns and sauses.

Yummy soup is ready, Enjoy it!

2ndRecipe:

Cappuccino Coffee:

Ingredients:

  • Coffee powder.
  • Sugar.
  • Hot Milk.
  • Hot water.
  • Chocolate syrup.

Method To Cook:

  1. Take 2 tbsp of coffee powder and 3 tbsp of sugar in a mug.
  2. Add some hot water and mix or beat it well till coffee change its colour and become thick.
  3. after mixing, Add hot milk and stir it well.
  4. at last, add some chocolate syrup on top for garnish.

Hot cappuccino coffee is ready to take.

3rdRecipe:

Chicken and vegetable macaroni:

Ingredients:

  • 250 gm boiled macaroni.
  • 1 small cabbage.
  • 1 capsicum.
  • 150 gm peas.
  • 1 carrot.
  • Some spring onion.
  • Spices:
    • Garam masala.
    • Red chilli powder.
    • ginger and garlic powder.
    • Black pepper.
    • Salt.
    • Lemon juice.
  • Boiled and shredded chicken.
  • Oil.

Method To Cook:

  1. Take some oil in a pan.
  2. Add all vegetables with some water and cook them until they soften half.
  3. Add all spices, mix them and cover it till all vegetables will completely cook.
  4. Roast them.
  5. Add chicken and mix it completely with all vegetables.
  6. Add boiled macaroni.
  7. At last, Add some lemon juice.

Tasty macaroni is ready to eat!
